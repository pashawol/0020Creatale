Area Onety Five

You thought you’d hit it big with the apprenticeship at Area Onety Five, but then the invasion came… You pillaged a random science lab and have no idea what all this stuff does. Probably break really soon. And then what do you do? Use [WASD] to run und jump, [I] to open your inventory and left click to shoot.

Game type

Jump'n'Run, Singleplayer, 2D, Keyboard/Mouse input

Features
  • WebGL accelerated sprite graphics with pixie.js
  • Infinitely generated level
  • 12 Wacky Weapons

Our entry for the Global Game Jam 2014. Thanks to our artists Sonja Seiler, Sarah S. Stark, and Christian Teller.
